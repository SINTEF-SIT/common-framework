Notes: Cabotage is the transport of goods or passengers between two points in the same country by a transport means registered in another country. Originally a shipping term, cabotage now also covers aviation, railways, and road transport.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>For maritime, the EU cabotage rules (regulation 3577/92) ensure that maritime transport services within a Member State, i.e. purely national connections, can be offered by companies of other Member States.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>For road transport, cabotage (meaning the national carriage of goods for hire or reward carried out by non resident hauliers on a temporary basis in a host Member State) is governed by Regulation (EC) 1072/2009<br /></p>
