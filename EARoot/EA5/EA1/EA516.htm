The Logistics Service Provider is the role that plans, markets and provides logistics services. The requirements for the services are collected from the Logistics Service Client. The Logistics Service Provider also communicates with the Transportation Network Manager and the Regulator during planning and execution of the logistics services in order to receive and communicate infrastructure information and regulatory information respectively. The Logistics Service Provider is responsible for providing the Logistics Service Client with status information during the execution of the transport operation. The Logistics Service Provider role can be performed by for example carriers, warehouses or terminals, or as mentioned above a forwarder or freight service integrator. The commonality is that they provide logistics services (single services or door-to-door services).
