The Logistics Operations Manager is responsible for the planning and execution management of the logistics operations. The planning includes resource allocations, time schedule planning, etc. and is done across all the available logistics demands from different Logistics Service Clients. The logistics operations may be transport between location; logistics unit handling (loading, unloading, consolidations, etc.); warehouse operations; document handling related to logistics units (e.g. customs), etc. Logistics operations must be carried out according to rules and regulations.<br /></p><p>The management includes monitoring and follow up of the transport operation execution carried out by the Logistics Operation Worker, required reporting to Regulators, etc. <br /></p>
