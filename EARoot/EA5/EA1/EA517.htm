The Transportation Network Manager is the role that makes information regarding the infrastructure and the traffic situation needed for the planning and executing of logistics operations available to the Logistics Service Client and Logistics Service Provider. This role can be performed by for example private information service providers or public traffic information centres or road/coastal/rail administrations.
