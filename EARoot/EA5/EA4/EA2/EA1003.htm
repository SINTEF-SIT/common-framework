This process is about the strategic planning of the traffic management policy for different day and time periods (e.g. rush hours). Normal variations in the traffic situation must be considered and handled. The plan must consider for example statistics about traffic and transport demands (e.g. number of vehicles), information about the pre-defined routes of the Logistics Service Providers.
