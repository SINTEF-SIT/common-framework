Appears In: Optimisation of resources, BLI: iCargo Business Level Innovations, Manage Use of Logistics Services
Here the Logistics Service Client plans and prepares the use of logistics services with respect to defining terms and conditions, collecting relevant logistics service information from long term contracts or via the Transport Service Description (TSD) interface, and selecting and booking logistics services via the Transport Execution Plan (TEP) interface. Once the services are agreed upon, the Logistics Service Manager may issue Goods Itinerary Information (via the GII interface) to provide itinerary information for the goods, i.e. details on routes, time schedules, etc.
