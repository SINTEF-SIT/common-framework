The process addresses preparation, planning, execution management and completion management of logistics services. The preparation and planning may start at an early stage and may be an iterative process with several re-planning loops where the Transport Execution Plan (TEP) is refined and changed before it is ready for execution. Even TEPs that are agreed upon (the TEP OK condition in the diagram) may be changed before execution.<br /></p><p>If the planning is finalized long before the start of the execution, the process will wait for either a re-planning, a cancellation or the start of the execution.<br /></p><p>The TEP may also be changed during execution. The execution is then interrupted, and the planning loop is re-entered.<br /></p>
