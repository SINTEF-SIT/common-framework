Initially, the Logistics Service Manager receives a service request from the Logistics Service Client via the Transport Execution Plan (TEP) interface. The TEP information is forwarded to the Logistics Operations Manager who does the initial planning of the actual transport operations. Based on the result of this planning, a TEP response is given to the Logistics Service Client. <br /></p><p>Before and during the execution of the transport service, updates of the TEP may be initiated both from the Logistics Service Client and the Logistics Operation Manager. Thus, new TEPs may have to be exchanged at any time. <br /></p>
