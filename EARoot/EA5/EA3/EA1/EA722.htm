Appears In: Manage Logistics Service Strategies
Strategic decisions on how to provide logistics services and how to handle special conditions are made. This may for example be related to<br /></p><p><ul><li>Priorities of service requests</li><br /></p><p><li>How to handle deviations</li><br /></p><p><li>How to handle incidents</li><br /></p><p></ul>The consequences with respect to the efficiency of the logistics operations must be considered. It may for example be relevant to adapt to local traffic management policies (decided by the Transport Network Management). Priority and access policies in cities may for example regulate the access to areas of cities dependent on type of engine (e.g. environmental friendly), type of cargo (e.g. dangerous cargo), load factor, planned destination, etc. <br /></p>
