This process focuses around the communication with the Logistics Service Client. I principle this can be divided into three phases: Management of service requests by means of Transport Execution Plans (TEPs); management of the execution of services; and completion of the service provisions. Since the TEPs may be changed at any time, the management of service request will go on in parallel with the others, and the management of the service execution will at any time be according to the valid TEP. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>In addition to the above, the Goods Item Itinerary may, if requested by the client, also be maintained according to the at any time valid plans for the execution of the logistics services and according to the actual execution of them. Such a Goods Item Itinerary may describe several journey segments.<br /></p>
