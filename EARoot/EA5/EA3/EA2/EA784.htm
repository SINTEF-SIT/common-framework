The planning is a continuous process going on in parallel with the execution of all the current logistics operations. All available Transport Execution Plans are considered as well as transport network information, traffic information and specific information on the mobility of specific transport means. The latter is received via the Transport Progress Status (TPS) interface from the Transport Network Manager. When the transport operations are planned, Goods Item Itinerary (GII) information describing the plans for the execution of the requested logistics services is established and provided to the Logistics Service Manager.
