This process address how the Logistics Operations Manager plans and follows up the execution of transport operations. The activities are going on in parallel. Information on new transport service bookings (TEP information) may come at any time, and these have to be considered by the continuous planning process. The aim is to plan and execute the most efficient and transport operations both with respect to the fulfillment of the demands from the clients, but also with respect to resource utilization, environmental impacts, costs, etc. It is important to bear in mind that one transport operation may cover one or more legs of the total transport demands requested by one or several TEPs. Note also that in the diagram there is just one Execute Logistics Operation activity. In reality there may be many such activities – one for each ongoing operation.
