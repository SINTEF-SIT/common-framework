The actual execution of a transport operation is managed. The transport network resources needed (e.g. loading bays, terminal areas, etc.) may be booked, and priorities and access to specific part of the transport network may be requested if this is required. The Transport Operation Plans (TOPs) are established and provided to the Logistics Operations Workers involved. During the execution of the transport operations, these workers may provide status information to the Logistics Service Manager. On-board Managers, who operate the transport means, may need route guidance and the transport means may also be tracked. The Goods Item Itinerary information related to the Transport Execution Plans affected by the logistic operation is updated to reflect how the requested logistics services are carried out.
