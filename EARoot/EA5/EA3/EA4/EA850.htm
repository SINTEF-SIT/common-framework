Appears In: Manage Vehicle and Transport Operation Information
Vehicle and transport operation information is registered and managed. Relevant information is: The identity of the vehicle and vehicle properties (type of vehicle, type of engine, size of vehicle, weight of empty vehicle, etc.); overall information about the cargo (type of cargo on board, amount of cargo, description of hazardous materials, vehicle fill, vehicle weight when laden, etc.); the next destinations and related time slots and bookings of resources, e.g. loading bays.<br /></p><p>Relevant information may be reported to transport regulators whenever required.<br /></p>
