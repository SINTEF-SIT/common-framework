The Transport Operation Plan (TOP) is received and is used in the transport operation management. Relevant information is provided to the On-board Manager. Status information is received from the On-board management and from the monitoring and tracking activities (see below). The aggregated status information is provided to the Logistics Operation Manager. Transport network resources may be booked.
