The role belongs to the Logistics Demand domain of the Reference Model<br /></p><p>The Logistics Service Client is the role representing anyone that searches for logistics services; books logistics services and follows up the execution of logistics services. Thus, the Logistics Service Client provides the Logistics Service Provider with instructions and detailed information about the logistics units and cargo to be included in the logistics services. Both the consignor (sender) and the consignee (receiver) may act as a Logistics Service Client depending on how the businesses make their transport arrangements. This role can also be performed by for example shippers or receivers, or as mentioned above a forwarder or freight service integrator. The commonality is that they purchase logistics services from a Logistics Service Provider.<br /></p>
