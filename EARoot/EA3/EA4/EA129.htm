This also includes the use of abilities and resources operated by one or more Logistics Operation Managers. </li><br /></p><p><li>Marketing of the logistics services, including the publishing of information about available logistics services</li><br /></p><p></ul><ul><li>The customer relations (i.e. contact with the Logistics Service Clients).
