The Logistics Operations Manager is responsible for<br /></p><p><br /></p><p><ul><li>Decision on how to fulfil the  logistics demand by means of one or more logistics operations, e.g. transport from one point to another; logistics unit handling (e.g. at terminal); warehouse operations; document handling related to logistics units  (e.g. customs), etc.</li><br /></p><p><li>The planning of the logistics operations (resource utilisation, time schedule, etc.). that can be carried out according to rules and regulations.</li><br /></p><p><li>The management of ongoing logistics operations, including monitoring and follow up of the logistics operations carried out by the Logistics Operation Worker and the use of Transport Means (in Vehicle Control and Support).</li><br /></p><p><li>Reporting related to logistics operations (e.g. to authorities); etc. (if not done by the On-board Management domain)</li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p>
